Google Rules: AEO and GEO Are Still Just SEO

developers.google.com

Published May 15, Google's official AI Optimization Guide is its first documented stance on AEO and GEO: 'Optimizing for generative AI search is optimizing for the search experience, and thus still SEO.' Google explicitly lists tactics that do not apply to AI Mode and AI Overviews: content chunking, llms.txt files, AI-specific rewriting, and special schema markup for generative features.

Published May 15, 2026
POST
WHY IT MATTERS

This kills the AEO/GEO consulting category built over the past 18 months. For GTM engineers running content pipelines: stop optimizing AI Mode as a separate channel. The signal is to double down on E-E-A-T and structured data — the fundamentals that have always worked. Kill any workflow built around llms.txt or AI chunking.
